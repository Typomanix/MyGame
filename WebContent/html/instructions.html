Instructions

Typomanix is a type racing game which test your ability of typing. There is an area provided where the player has to type as fast as he can to score well.

Type as many words as you can before the time runs out.


Playing instructions:

Step 1 : Login into the game.
Step 2 : Click on Play Game option.
Step 3 : Select Category of your choice.
Step 4 : Click on Lets begin.
Step 5 : Choose the level according to ur comfort level. (HINT : Start with easy level)
Step 6 : Now start typing the words as it appears on screen before the timer goes off
Step 7 : Once the timer goes off the game will end.
Step 8 : You can Quit the game in between of the game.
Step 9 : You can Reset the game if want to play again.

(HINT : To understand the flow of game Play as guest first)
